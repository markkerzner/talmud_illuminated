<div>
If the owner impudently walked away and sat down, saying to the bystander, - "Since the commandment to unload my animal is incumbent upon you, if you wish to unload, then unload, but I shall not help you," - the bystander is exempt, for it is stated "...<a href="http://bible.cc/exodus/23-5.htm">you shall help with him</a>..."</div><div><br /></div><div>Art: Bearing a Burden by Mortimer Ludington Menpes</div>
