<span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: medium;"><br /></span></div>
<div>
<span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: medium;">In large cities, where there is a perennial housing shortage, a landlord must give twelve months' notice before evicting a tenant. </span></div>
<div>
<span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: medium;"><br /></span></div>
<div>
<span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: medium;">With regard to stores, both in towns and large cities, a landlord must give twelve months' notice to allow the storeowners to start collecting on the credit they usually extend to their customers. </span></div><div><span class="Apple-style-span" style="font-size: medium;"><br /></span></div><div><span style="font-size: medium;">Art: A House in Winter by Henri Eugene Augustin Le Sidaner</span></div>
