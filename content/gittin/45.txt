If a man gives his sister a ring in marriage (which all know to be invalid),  Rav says, he gave it for safekeeping to be returned, but Shmuel says, he gave it as a present to keep.<br />
<br />
If a slave runs away to Israel, he is not returned to his master.<br />
<br />
One should not ransom captives (with some exceptions) for much more than their worth. Is it not to burden the community, or not to accustom the captors to gain? - Unresolved.<br />
<br />
One should not ransom tefillin or Torah for more than their worth nor should one provoke with low offer.
