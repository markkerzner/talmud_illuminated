Gittin 90 (Conclusion) - Reasons for Divorce

<br />
But Beit Hillel say, “Even if she burned his dish.” Proof: “Adulterous or any other matter.”<br />
<br />
Rabbi Akiva says, “Even if he found another more attractive than her.” Proof: “And it comes to pass that she does not find favor in his eyes.<br />
<br />
Despised by God is anyone who sends away his wife without a compelling reason.<br />
<br />
Whenever anyone divorces his first wife, even the <a href="http://bible.cc/malachi/2-13.htmhttp://bible.cc/malachi/2-13.htm">Temple Altar sheds tears on his account</a>.</span>
