Is one sells his slave to an idolater , the slave is freed as a penalty to his owner, because the slave will have to violate commandments. Same for selling the slave to outside of Israel, because of the precept against leaving Israel.<br />
<br />
If one fertilizes his field in the seventh year, then as a penalty he can not sow it next year; however, if he dies, his sons can sow that field.<br />
