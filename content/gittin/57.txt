Gittin 57 - Gladden Jerusalem
"Gladden Jerusalem and rejoice with her all those who love her; be elated with her, all those who mourn for her." (Isaiah) means: when Jerusalem is in the state of joy, one must rejoice, and when it is in mourning, one must mourn.<br />

She was a reincarnation of the mother of Abraham.
