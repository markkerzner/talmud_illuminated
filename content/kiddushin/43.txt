<div>
The two exceptions are misappropriation of Temple property and stealing and selling of an animal, for which the sender is liable. </div>
<div>
<br /></div>
<div>
If one says to his agent, "Go and kill a certain person," the agent is liable for the deed, while the one who sent him is exempt. Shammai says, "The one who sent him is liable" based on "...you have killed him with the sword of the Ammonites."</div><div><br /></div><div>Art: A Murder by Cezanne</div>
