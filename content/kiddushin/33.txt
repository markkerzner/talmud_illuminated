The Torah continues, "Fear your God," meaning one should not avert his eyes pretending he did not see the sage.<br />
<br />
One is not obligated to suffer monetary loss by standing up in front of a sage; even so, one is obligated to stand up in front of a person bringing the first fruit to Jerusalem. Why is this? Because a mitzvah in its proper time is especially beloved.<div><br /></div><div>Art:&nbsp;Paying Respects to His High Mightiness</div>
