Bava Batra 15 – Who Wrote the Books of Scripture?

<span style="font-size: 130%;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: 130%;">Samuel wrote his book, and the Book of Judges and Ruth. David wrote the Book of Psalms, where he included the work of ten other authors.</span><br />
<span style="font-size: 130%;"><br /></span>
<span style="font-size: 130%;">Hezekiah and his assistants wrote the books of Isaiah, Proverbs, Song of Songs and Ecclesiastes; the Men of the Great Assembly – a group of 120 Sages active during the first years of the Second Temple – wrote the Books of Ezekiel, the Twelve Prophets, Daniel, and the Scroll of Esther.</span>
