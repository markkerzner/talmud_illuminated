<div>
<br /></div>
<div>

<div>
It reverts to "innocent" if it is confronted by circumstances that triggered goring the first three times yet now desists from this behavior on three separate days. Rabbi Meir adds that it reverts to "innocent" any time the children poke it between the horns and does not gore.</div>
