<br />
Says Rabbi Shimon HaTimni: “an extra word fist teaches us that just as a fist, the weapon must be available for inspection,” - but Rabbi Akiva disagrees. However, they agree that an assessment is required: the court must asses that the injury was probable and not an accident.<br />
<br />
If one humiliates someone with words, he is not liable for any payment, however, the offender may loose his share in the World to Come.<br />
<br />
It is forbidden to cut down valuable fruit trees.<br />
