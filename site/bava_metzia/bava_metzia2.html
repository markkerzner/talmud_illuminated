Bava Metzia 2 - Two People Found a Garment

Two litigants came before the court holding on to a cloak. One says "I found it first," and the other one says "I found it first." One says, "It is mine," and the other one says, "It is mine." One must swear that he owns no less than half of it, and other one must swear that he owns no less that half of it, and they then divide it - if possible, or sell it and divide the money.
Why claim "It is mine?" It's another case, where they both gave money to the storekeeper, and now argue whose is the article.
